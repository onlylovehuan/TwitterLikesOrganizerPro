# Privacy Policy

Twitter Likes Organizer Pro ("the Extension") is designed to help users organize and categorize their liked tweets on Twitter/X.

## Data Collection

- **Local Storage Only**: All data is stored locally on your device. The extension does not upload your data to any external server.
- **Tweet Content**: When you use the extension to capture liked tweets, the content (text, images, videos, links, author information) is saved to your browser's local storage.
- **API Keys**: If you configure AI categorization with OpenAI or Claude API, your API key is stored locally and never shared with us or any third parties.

## Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for:
- Displaying and organizing your liked tweets
- AI-powered categorization (when API is configured)
- Your personal reference and organization

We do not use your data for:
- Advertising or marketing purposes
- Selling to third parties
- Any purpose unrelated to the extension's core functionality

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share your data with any third parties, except:
- When you explicitly configure an AI API (OpenAI/Claude), tweet content is sent to those services for categorization purposes only
- As required by law

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your stored data at any time
- Delete all your data by using the "Clear All" function in the extension
- Export your data in JSON format for backup

## Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact the developer through the extension's support channels.
