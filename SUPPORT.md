# Support

  ## Getting Help

  If you encounter any issues or have questions about Twitter Likes Organizer Pro, please:

  1. Check the GitHub Issues page for known problems
  2. Create a new issue if your problem hasn't been reported
  3. Provide detailed information about your issue

  ## FAQ

  **Q: Why can't I capture my liked tweets?**
  A: Make sure you're on twitter.com or x.com and have liked tweets visible on your profile.

  **Q: Is my data secure?**
  A: Yes, all data is stored locally on your device.

  ## Contact

  For additional support, please open an issue on GitHub.
